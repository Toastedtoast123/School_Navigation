Welcome to ICCT Navigation

A quick, beginner-friendly guide to get you moving.

Step 1 of 4
1 Getting Started
🧭 Look for the menu to navigate.

This site helps you find places around campus and plan routes. Start by using the navigation bar to open Rooms.

  • 📍 Rooms → explore each room page.
  • 🧠 Follow the steps to learn how to go from Start to Destination.
  • 🖱️ Everything is responsive—works on mobile too.
☰ Top Navigation
2 Choose Places
🎯 Pick your start & end.

Open a room and use the route controls to set your From and To inputs.

A
From
Your starting location on campus.
B
To
Your destination room/area.
➡️
Tip
Type carefully—use suggestions if available. Then confirm and view the route.
3 View the Route
🧱 Follow the path on the map.

After you set both locations, the route appears on the campus viewer. Take a moment to scan the highlighted path.

🗺️ Map Viewer
🪄
Navigation
Zoom/pan if supported. If the route looks off, revisit your From/To selection.
4 Move Confidently
✅ Ready to go!

You’re set. Now you can explore different rooms and plan routes anytime.

  • 🔁 Re-run a route by changing inputs.
  • 🏢 Use Rooms to jump to another location.
  • 📱 The experience adapts to your device for a smooth walkthrough.
One last thing
When you’re ready, hit Get Started to close the tutorial.
